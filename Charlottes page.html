Vegan Ingredients & Alternatives

Our top 10 ingrediants and food alternatives to lead a Vegan life!


Thinking of going vegan, or including more vegan options to your repertoire? Switching to a vegan diet can be daunting, but to ease the transition, here are some accessible substitutes.

  • Milk - Popular non-dairy milks include oat, hazelnut, cashew, soy, almond and hemp. With so many non-dairy alternatives to choose from you are bound to find one to tickle your tastebuds! Non- dairy milks are fairy accessible now in large supermarket stores but if you find yourself struggling in the supermarket aisles, look no further than this easy guide to make your own!
  • Butter - As well as vegan butters available, margerine serves as an excellent alternative to butter.
  • Meat - Thanks to our veggie friends, meat substitutes are generally very accessible in everyday supermarkets. Among the products on the market now that will substitute for meat are: veggie burgers, veggie meatballs, veggie sausages, veggie ground “beef,” soy chicken patties and nuggets and even veggie bacon!
